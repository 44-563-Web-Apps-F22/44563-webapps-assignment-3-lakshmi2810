MountVernon Magnus

Magnus, the mascot for Cleveland State University athletics, has advanced to the Elite 8 of the Mid-Major Madness Mascot Melee.

  1. CSU's current mascot Magnus was introduced in 2007 and his name was decided via an online poll.
  2. A welcome return to a human mascot following the horrors of Vike.
  3. Magnus is a man of few words, instead relying on his piercing stare and permanent smile to intimidate students and rivals alike.
